## About his carachteristics

- **Dimension of standing**: 70cm × 31cm × 40cm
- **Material**: Aluminium alloy + High strength engineering plastic
- **Weight (with battery)**: About 15kg
- **Voltage**: 28V ~ 33.6V
- **Payload**: ≈ 8kg (MAX ~ 12kg)
- **Speed**: 0 ~ 3.7m/s (MAX ~ 5m/s)
- **Max Climb Angle**: 40°
- **Basic Computing Power**: 8-core High-performance CPU RK3588S
- **Max Torque**: About 45N·m
- **Battery Capacity**: standard (8000mAh) / long endurance (15000mAh)
- **Battery Life**: 1–2h / 2–4h
