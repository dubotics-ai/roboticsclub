# Let`s Jailbreak!

<Image src="/jailbreak_pawroot.jpg" alt="jailbreak" width={800} height={800} />

- Download the jailbreak binary: [https://disk.yandex.com/d/r2EjmqFmIPC9vw](https://disk.yandex.com/d/r2EjmqFmIPC9vw) and rename it to "jailbreak"
- Connect the dog in STA_L mode to your local network(that means, connect the robot on your wifi) and ensure your PC is on the same network.
- Make sure mobile APP is closed
- Run ./jailbreak and follow the on-screen instructions.
```bash
chmod +x jailbreak
./jailbreak
```
- Once the script is completed, REBOOT your dog norbmally, and wait for the dog to boot up.
- Enjoy your root access by ssh-ing under root, the password is ```theroboverse```:
```bash
ssh root@[your_dog_ip]
```
- To enable secondary development and do a backup on AIR/PRO follow the instructions in [go2_firmware_tools](https://github.com/legion1581/go2_firmware_tools/tree/1.1.1)
